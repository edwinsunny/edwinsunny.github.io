Specialist

Specialist is an app that offers every kind of expert professional whenever a person is in need.
Nowadays finding a skilled specialist when needed is very tough. For example, when my pipe got broken and water is splashing everywhere I don’t know what to do in that situation, or my car is not starting suddenly in the morning, my PC starts to act strange, etcetera. Even if we find anyone it would be really costly. This app is trying to solve this issue by bringing various specialists under one umbrella cost-efficiently. You can find different experts like Plumber, Mechanic, Car washers, Maid, Baby Sitters, Computer technicians, Electricity technicians, etcetera in a single app.

 
Fitness Goals (Android)

We are introducing new fitness app from which user can do various activities. In this modern time when people are very busy they don't have time for their health. So we are thinking to make an mobile app which will guide them for quick exercise and yoga which will keep them fit without going to gym and spending any money. This app will be free of cost for everyone. In this app there will be animation used for guiding people how to do the exercises and also written time for how long they have to do the raps and how many sets. In this app age will be asked so the person do the exercise according to their age. Age will be start from 6 years old.
